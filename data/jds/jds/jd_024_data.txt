PointClickCare is hiring a 

Remote PointClickCare Associate Data Scientist

We are looking for an Associate Data Scientist (or Data Analyst or Data Scientist, depending on experience) to join our Data Science department. Position Summary: The position is primarily aimed at analysis and reporting for our internal Product department. Our products generally focus on providing insights from hospital and skilled nursing facilities (SNFs) data to a variety of customers including hospitals, health systems, health plans, ACOs, and other similar health or data exchange entities. This role will be responsible for using real-time and historical healthcare data and user data to investigate new product opportunities and determine the health and user behavior of our current products. The main skillsets include extracting, cleaning, and preparing data; summarizing, visualizing, and interpreting data for a range of customer personas; and providing recommendations to Product personnel, Data Science personnel, management, other departments, and external clients
